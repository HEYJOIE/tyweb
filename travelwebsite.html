Travel website

by taiyang Nov 14th, 2016

This is my project of "Code-first" session, which is a travel website. The inspiration comes from my travel stories of my friend. We both like travelling and our dream is to travel around the world together and feel different views of the world. On the other hand, we want to record and share our stories to more and more people to encourage them to travel on the way, using the eyes and worrds to share the beautiful moments of our life. So I build a travel website to record our stories.
